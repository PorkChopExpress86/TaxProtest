Download Harris County Appraisal Data

The data used here is pulled from HCAD's database once a day.

Enter the data into the form to download data for your house and houses near you to compare the market, land and improvement values. Enter your account number, full or partial, street name, or zip code and click submit.

Hints:

  • Account Number: enter the first 5 or 6 numbers to get all houses very close by.
  • Street: To narrow the search to your street enter a space before and after your street. There may still be another street in Harris county with your street name, so pair it with your zip code.
  • Zip Code: pair it with your street name to ensure that ONLY your street is selected or get your entire zip code!
